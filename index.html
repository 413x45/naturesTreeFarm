About Us

Nature's Tree Farm is located just south of Atlanta, on 100 gently rolling acres. Here, we specialize in growing ornamental shade trees, sizes ranging from 7G to 45G. We also grow a fine selection of popular evergreens and flowering shrubs. Maples, Oaks, Magnolias, River Birch, Crape myrtles and more can be found at the Farm, all being carefully tended to by our professional staff. The varieties we grow have been meticulously selected to ensure they thrive in the unique climate and soils of the Southeast.

We frequently add new varieties to our inventory, so if what you’re looking for isn’t on the availability list, or if you simply have some question about our plants, please give us a call. In addition, if we can improve in any way, kindly let us know. We are happy to help and love satisfied customers!
